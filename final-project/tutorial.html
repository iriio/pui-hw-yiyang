Web Synthesizer User Manual

Quick Start Guide

  1. Click anywhere on the page to start the synthesizer
  2. Use your computer keyboard to play notes (keys A-L)
  3. Press numbers 1-4 to play drum sounds
  4. Press spacebar to start/stop the sequencer

Playing the Synthesizer

Keyboard Layout

The synthesizer can be played using your computer keyboard:

  • White keys: A S D F G H J K L ; '
  • Black keys: Q W E R T Y U I O P
  • Drum pads: 1 (Kick), 2 (Snare), 3 (Hi-hat), 4 (Clap)

Sound Controls

Oscillators

Your synthesizer has two main sound generators (oscillators) and a sub-oscillator for deep bass:

  • Oscillator 1 & 2: Choose between different waveforms:
    • Sine: Smooth, pure tone
    • Square: Rich, hollow sound
    • Sawtooth: Bright, buzzy sound
    • Triangle: Soft, mellow tone
  • Mix: Blend between the two oscillators
  • Sub Level: Add deep bass by turning up the sub-oscillator

Sound Shaping

Shape your sound using these controls:

  • Filter:
    • Frequency: Controls brightness (turn down for darker sounds)
    • Resonance: Adds a whistling quality at the filter frequency
  • Drive: Adds distortion and grit to your sound

Using the Sequencer

Basic Operation

  • Press spacebar or the Play button to start/stop
  • Adjust tempo using the BPM controls
  • Click any step to select it for recording
  • Press the Reverse button to play the sequence backwards

Recording Patterns

To record a pattern:

  1. Select a step by clicking it
  2. Play notes using your keyboard or click the on-screen keys
  3. Each step can hold up to 3 notes
  4. Click any recorded note to delete it
Tip: Hold Shift while playing notes to add them to multiple steps in sequence!

Keyboard Shortcuts

Key Function
Space Start/Stop sequencer
Shift + Note Add note to multiple steps
1 - 4 Play drum sounds
Double-click Toggle sticky notes
