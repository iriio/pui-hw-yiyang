Main Waveform Output

Oscillator

Controls the basic sound generation. Type changes the wave shape, Mix blends between two oscillators, and Sub adds a lower octave.

Filter

Shapes the tone of the sound. Cutoff controls brightness, Resonance emphasizes frequencies around the cutoff point.

Envelope

Controls how the sound changes over time. Attack is the fade-in time, Decay and Sustain control the middle portion, Release is the fade-out time.

Effects

Add character to the sound. Distortion adds grit, Delay creates echoes, and Width controls stereo spread.
